SBHE Project Management

An application built on Laravel and AngularJS used by company employees and owners to oversee and manage projects and employee work. It records all ongoing construction projects and tracks workers, including their attendance and hours. The application also maintains equipment backlogs and conditions, providing a comprehensive view of company resources and project status.
